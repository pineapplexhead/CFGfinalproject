Dusty Reyes
Dusty is the head of housekeeping at R&R’s bed and breakfast. She has two children, Lewis and Hazel, with her ex-husband Walter who is the manager of the B&B. As well as her housekeeping tasks, she is often busy helping guests with requests

“Only yesterday morning, Mr Green was asking me for some rope! I get some very specific requests from our guests. Thankfully I was able to find some for him in the shed.”

Dusty is very chatty, and she tells you lots of irrelevant information. Thankfully, after some time you get her back onto the subject and she tells you…
“I am ashamed to admit that I broke off my marriage with Walter after I slept with Charles several times. This was over 20 years ago and believe me, I am a different person now. I would never do something like that again and hurt my family. Thankfully, I don’t think Walter ever found out… we may not be together anymore, but I still care for him, and I fear that the affair would have broken his heart for good.”
Lying around is an old, battered crime novel. You are not sure if anyone is reading it- it may well have been left behind by one of the B&B guests. Flipping through it, the first page catches your eye...
